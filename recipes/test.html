Frap Roast

  • Into pitcher: 2L cold water, 1 packet frap roast
  • Whisk to mix

Iced Coffee

  • Brew iced coffee on 1/2 batch
  • Measure 2L iced coffee, then pour into dispenser. Discard the rest of the coffee in the urn
  • Measure 2L ice, then pour into dispenser
  • Whisk to mix

Cold Brew

  • Grind 5lb bag of cold brew on coarse setting
  • Insert filter bag into cold brewer basket
  • Insert 2 cold brew concentrate containers into bottom of unit
  • Fill filter bag with ground cold brew; cover with water trough tray
  • Measure and pour 14L cold water through the water trough tray; cover unit
  • Push button on unit once, or until 20 hours shows on screen
  • Day dot cold brew concentrate containers for 7 days from now

Keg

  • Pour 5L of chilled water into keg
  • Pour 5L of chilled cold brew concentrate into keg
  • Apply seal and flip upside down to check for leakage
  • Transfer day dot from cold brew concentrate to keg

Lemonade

  • Pour lemonade into 2L pitcher to the 0.75L line
  • Fill pitcher with cold water to the 2L line
  • Whisk to mix

Iced Tea

  • Place tea bag into pitcher, fill with hot water to the 1L line
  • Steep 5 minutes, remove bag with tongs
  • Add 1 venti scoop of ice
  • Fill to 2L line with cold water

Refresher Base

  • Pour carton into 2L pitcher (will fill to 1L line)
  • Fill to 2L line with cold water
  • Whisk to mix

Sweet Cream

  • Use a cold foam blender to measure 350mL of vanilla, pour into 2L pitcher
  • Use a cold foam blender to measure 600mL of 2% milk, pour into 2L pitcher
  • 1L heavy cream, pour into 2L pitcher
  • Whisk to mix
Note: this recipe produces 1.95L of sweet cream and therefore will not fill a 2L pitcher, this is by design

Whipped Cream

  • Use a steaming pitcher to measure 4 pumps of vanilla
  • Add heavy cream to the max line, pour into whip canister
  • Add lid with gasket, charge and shake 10 times
  • Remove charger before storing in fridge

Mocha

  • Into a mocha pitcher, measure 1L hot water
  • Add mocha powder to water and mix and create slurry
  • Add 1L hot water
  • Whisk to mix
