Sarnia Plays Here

Discover The Arena, Sarnia's exclusive two-story laser tag destination. With laser tag, rock climbing, and mini golf, thrill-seekers find non-stop excitement here.

Laser Tag

Elevate Your Game!

At our arena, we take laser tag to the next level by offering a two-storey playing space. Prepare to be amazed as you explore the multiple levels, corridors, and strategic vantage points that our arena has to offer. With two storeys of intense gameplay, you'll find yourself fully immersed in an adrenaline-pumping adventure like never before.

Rock Climb

Reach New Heights!

Embark on an exhilarating adventure and test your skills with our challenging rock climbing wall, designed to push your limits and provide a thrilling experience. As you ascend the towering wall, each handhold and foothold presents a new obstacle to conquer, demanding strength, focus, and determination. Feel the adrenaline course through your veins as you reach new heights, overcoming challenges and celebrating personal achievements.

Mini Golf

Putt a Smile On!

If you're looking for a more laid-back and leisurely activity, swing into a friendly round of mini golf. Engage in a delightful game that combines precision, strategy, and a dash of friendly competition. Navigate through the vibrant and creatively designed course, carefully maneuvering your way around obstacles, slopes, and tricky curves. Put your putting skills to the test as you aim for the elusive hole-in-one or engage in friendly banter with your companions as you laugh and enjoy the light-hearted atmosphere.
