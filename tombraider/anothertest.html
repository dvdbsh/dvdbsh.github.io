Media Description

Tomb Raider explores the intense origin story of Lara Croft and her ascent from a young woman to a hardened survivor. Armed only with raw instincts and the ability to push beyond the limits of human endurance, Lara must fight to unravel the dark history of a forgotten island to escape its relentless hold.

Cheats

PC Cheat Codes

  • God Mode

    Type "IDDQD" to become invincible.

  • Unlimited Ammo

    Type "IDKFA" to get unlimited ammunition.

  • Unlock All Weapons

    Type "IDFA" to unlock all weapons.

PS3 Cheat Codes

  • Infinite Health

    Hold L1 + R1 and press Up, Down, Left, Right, Square.

  • Max Ammo

    Hold L1 + R1 and press Circle, Circle, Triangle, Triangle, Up.

  • Unlock All Levels

    Hold L1 + R1 and press Left, Right, Left, Right, Circle.

Xbox 360 Cheat Codes

  • God Mode

    Hold LB + RB and press Up, Down, Left, Right, X.

  • Unlimited Ammo

    Hold LB + RB and press B, B, Y, Y, Up.

  • Unlock All Weapons

    Hold LB + RB and press Left, Right, Left, Right, B.
