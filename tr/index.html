Tomb Raider 2013 overview

Tomb Raider follows Lara Croft in her first expedition as a young archaeologist. Stranded on the lost island of Yamatai, she must transform from an unsure academic into a survivor who can challenge the cult that rules the island.

Cheats and extras

PC extras

  • Explorer kit

    Unlocks a survival skin and an early access bow upgrade for Lara.

  • Scavenger pack

    Provides bonus salvage to speed up early weapon upgrades.

Console tips

  • Camp fast travel

    Use base camps to return to earlier areas and clean up collectibles.

  • Quick melee finishers

    Stagger enemies with arrows, then use finishers to save ammo.

Completion hints

  • Relic hunter

    Study relics from the menu to reveal extra clues about Yamatai.

  • Tomb raider

    Optional tombs reward large amounts of salvage and experience.
